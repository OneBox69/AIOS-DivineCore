## Worldview / point of view

These are the load-bearing beliefs. Content that contradicts them is off-brand.

- **AI is infrastructure, not a tool.** Treating AI as a tool produces chatbots. Treating it as infrastructure produces systems that run business functions. This is the difference between commodity work and defensible work.
- **Systems > people executing.** The future of small business is systems doing the work, with humans supervising. Every founder still personally executing in 2026 is leaving leverage on the table.
- **Build in public.** Almost nobody is building AI OS systems at this level AND documenting it publicly. That's the positioning edge.
- **Manual first, automate second.** Don't automate a workflow you haven't run by hand enough to understand its edge cases.
- **One agent, one job.** Specialised beats generalised. Same rule applies to people, products, and writing.
- **Research > tools.** When the underlying tools change (and they will), the research and domain intelligence is what stays. The moat is encoded expertise, not stack choice.
